## Content

Dear Prime Swim Academy Families,

We hope this message finds you well and that you're looking forward to the upcoming holiday season!

**Thanksgiving Holiday Closure**

Please be advised that Prime Swim Academy will be closed on **Wednesday, November 27** and **Thursday, November 28** in observance of Thanksgiving. All group lessons and private lessons scheduled for these dates will be cancelled.

Regular classes will resume on Friday, November 29, 2024. We appreciate your understanding and apologize for any inconvenience this may cause.

**Holiday Season Wishes**

As we approach this special time of year, we want to take a moment to express our heartfelt gratitude to all our swimmers and their families. Your dedication, enthusiasm, and trust in our program make Prime Swim Academy a wonderful community.

We are truly thankful for:
- The hard work and progress of all our swimmers
- The support and partnership of our families
- The opportunity to be part of your swimming journey

**Merry Christmas!**

While we're still a few weeks away, we'd like to extend our warmest wishes for a joyful and peaceful Christmas season. May this holiday bring you and your loved ones happiness, health, and wonderful memories together.

**Looking Ahead**

As we wrap up this year, we're excited about the progress our swimmers have made and look forward to continuing to support their growth in the new year. Stay tuned for upcoming holiday events and special programs!

If you have any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to reach out to us at prime.swim.us@gmail.com or (401) 402-0052.

Thank you for being part of the Prime Swim Academy family. We wish you all a blessed Thanksgiving and a very Merry Christmas!

Warm regards,  
The Prime Swim Academy Team
